What it do

The Program

This program seek to enable a car to operate within an environment consisting of human driven and other autonomous vehicles, with reduce chances of collisions. Cars will be equipped with a vehicle collision avoidance system and this will lower the driver and passengers’ risk of meeting in an accident. The program will achieve this through driver alerts and automatic braking/lane assistance. This Program will mainly target older vehicles that did not come with a simisalr program.

How it work

How Our Program Works

Our Program is quite simple and easy to use.
01

Sensor Detection

Sensors are placed throughout the vehicle and they detect different objects and send the information to the system.

Alert Driver of Pending Collision

Once the system detects a pending Collision, it sends a Alert to the driver. This is useful in the cases where a person might have fallen asleep around the wheel or got distracted.
02
03

Failure to Alert Driver

If the alert fails to warn the Driver and the system detects no driving adjustment to avoid the collision, the system now prepares to make its own adjustments.

Slow or Stop Vehicle

After the failed alert, the system now tries to avoid a collision by Slowing down or stoping the vehicle.
04
05

Lane Assistance

Additionally, the system has a lane assistance feature which will make adjustments where necessary to keep a vehicle in its lane. (This feature is only available on properly marked road)

System Report

The system keeps a record of every instance where it had to make adjustments movement of the vehicle.

Why use it

Significance

The importance of this project stems from the possibility to decrease the total amount of fatalities and injuries annually from motor vehicle accidents. These accidents not only hurt the friends and loved ones of the victims but, it also has negative economic impacts. Collision avoidance systems can be a great way to help your vehicles steer clear of safety incidents while also serving as a valuable tool to train drivers on safety best practices. Because these monitoring systems use sensor technology to detect impending collisions, they’re able to alert drivers of a problem before it’s too late.
